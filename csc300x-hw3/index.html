Las Tejanitas

Las Tejanitas is a local, family owned trpoical dessert shop, located less than a quarter mile from the UNCG campus. They specialize in tasty fruit drinks and treats with a twist, often combing sweet and spicy flavors to create the perfect treats for a hot day. Below you'll find 3 EPIC menu tiems that will be sure to add Las Tejanitas to your list of best small businesses in Greensboro!
Hours - everyday, 12pm-8pm
Address - 1030 W Gate City Blvd, Greensboro, NC

Mangonada - $
The Mangonada is a delicous sweet and spicy treat that is sure to cool your thirst on a hot day. Ths drink is a blend of mango smoothie, chamoy, tahini, and delicious slices of fresh mango. The sweet and spicy flavors complement each other perfectly, making it a crowd favorite. Make sure to enjoy the spicy treat on the straw as well!
Cucumber Lime Aguas Frescas - $
The Cucumber Lime Aguas Frescas is a deliciously cool and sweet drink that combines the fresh taste of cucumber with the sweet tangy zest of a fresh lime. This drink is a favorite for a reason, it appeals to many people as it is not too sweet, and still has a strong flavor profile. This is the perfect drink for a summer day, or if you are just running errands and need something tasty to sip on.
Mexican Street Corn = $
Mexican Street Corn is a favorite as a side dish, tasty dessert, or as a midday snack. Its a versatile snack that combines sweet corn with spicy Hot Cheetos or Takis to create a flavor explosion. The delicious mayaonaise and cotija cheese that is mixed in with the corn, topped with a squeeze of lime makes the perfect snack for yourself or for sharing with friends.
HINT: Don't forget to ask for extra limes and cheetos for your Mexican Street Corn!

Jake's Billiards

Jake's Billiards is a fun bar/grill to enjoy a night out with your friends, grab takeout, or have a romantic date night at. Jake's brings together the contemporary night out ambiance with a classic bar and grill theme, offering billiards and dart boards to play with friends, and fun designs throughout the restaraunt. The food at Jake's is the best part, their classic dishes like hot dogs, burgers, and chicken sandwiches are elevated by thier excellent service and great tasting sauces, all at an affordable price! Jake's is a great place to hang out with friends and enjoy a night of great food and fun!
Hours - 11am-12am
Adddress - 1712 Spring Garden St, Greensboro, NC 27403-3197

Chicken Sandwich - $8.50
The Jake's Chicken Sandwich is a classic favorite from this establishment. With the variety of sauces to choose from, everyone can enjoy a perfectly cooked, high quality chicken sandwich with a flavor profile to thier liking. In addition, the toppings of lettuce and tomato, and a bit of honey mustard elevate this dish to the next level. Couple that will a side of tater tots fir just $8.50, WOW!
Veggie Dogs - $3.50
Jake's Veggie Dogs are well-known across Greensboro for thier discount on Mondays, at just $2 each! They come fully topped with ketchup, mustard, cole slaw, chilli, and onions, and can be elevated with a side of fries or tater tots! Even on the weekdays, they are still just $3.50, and are a tasty meal for lunch or dinner!
Turkey Burger - $9.00
The Jake's Turkey Burger is a great alternative to those who don't enjoy eating red meat, or just want to try something new! This delicious burger tastes great, fills you up, and comes with delicious sides like fries or tater tots!
HINT: The spicy flavor is the best sauce flavor for the Chicken Sandwich!

Sticks and Stones Clay Oven Pizza

Sticks and Stones is a modern, contemporary style pizzeria and eatery. They serve a diverse selection of pizzas, sandwiches, appetizers and desserts. The atmosphere is warm and the decor is beautiful. The tables are each topped with menu featuring thier dishes, and on the back you'll find adoptable dogs in the area. Sticks and Stones also has an outdoor seating area equipped with pet water bowls. They also offer takeout and online orders. The wait staff are always friendly. At Sticks and Stones you can find delicious food and an enjoyable environment for socializing.
Hours - 11am-11pm
Adddress - 2200 Walker Ave, Greensboro, NC 27403

Large Easy Plateau Pizza - $21.95
This delicious pizza packs a PUNCH! It has a little something for everyone, with its grass-fed ground beef, swiss chard, potatoes, mozzerlla, and goat lady chevre toppings, all sitting on top of a delicious tomato sauce. The beef isprefectly seasoned and cooked, and the potatoes are a fun addition that adds a lot of substance and flavor to the meal. This pizza is sure to be a surprise and a delight.
Large Mockingbird Sing Pizza - $21.95
The Mokcingbird Sing Pizza is a delicious chicken pizza topped with walut pesto, fennel, tomoatoes, spinach, mozzzerlla, and gorgonzola cheese. This pizza is light, tasty, and packs a dierse flavor profile that will leave you wanting more. This pizza has 2 delicious types of cheese that complement the chicken and spices, and is sure to delight.
Peach Cobber with Ice Cream - $5.95
The wood-oven baked peach cobbler topped with a scopp of vanilla ice cream is a great way to end your meal at Sticks and Stones. The cobbler is crunchy and sweet, and the warm cobbler is nicely complemented by the scoop of vanilla ice cream on top. Being wood-oven baked the dessert comes out crispy and perfectly cooked. A great way to end a delicious meal at sticks at Sticks and Stones.
HINT: Make sure to top your pizza with parmasean and red pepper flakes!

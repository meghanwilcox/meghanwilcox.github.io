Granny's Pie's

Welcome to Granny's Pies! We are a small bakery shop loated in Greensboro, NC. We have been in the businesss of PIES for a long time! We love baking delicious treats and sharing them with others. Our specialty is our world famous Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie Recipe! We also bake apple, peach, pumpkin, chocolate, pecan, blueberry, and key lime pies, so we have a little something for everyone! Our business started many years ago with Granny, who wasn't a Granny back then! Granny has always loved baking and sharing her talent with others! Now we are a multi-generational bake shop that keeps Granny's loved for baked goods alive in every pie we sell. Make sure to check out the variety of pies that we offer! And bring your friends and family to enjoy a delicious treat! Happy PIE shopping!
